Economic Recovery

We understand each business has been uniquely impacted by Covid-19 and we can support you as recover, maintain the norm and/or grow. We can:


  • Provide end to end strategic assessments and prioritisation recommendations
  • Provide Programme Management and Project Management solutions
  • Tailored services to support your needs

Risk

We can build, develop and/or enhance your enterprise risk management requirements. We can support with:


  • Risk Appetite Frameworks and Statements
  • Risk Culture Assessments
  • Risk Maturity Assessments
  • Strategic, Tactical or Day-to-Day Risk Management

Compliance

We help you to navigate and be prepared to demonstrate how you meet your regulatory obligations. We support with:


  • Obligations registers
  • Ongoing obligatory measurement, monitoring and reporting
  • Regulatory remediation assessments and tracking

Governance

We can assess your governance environment and structure and recommend best practise. We support with:


  • Management and Board capability assessments
  • Management and Board professional development workshops

Internal Audit and Assurance

We can deliver strategic business assurance, that provides focus for your business objectives which overall enhances your organisations performance.


  • Best practice assessments for people, process and technology
  • End to end assessments of different areas within your environment to ensure you are operating at your optimum and ensure you have the tools to address your blind spots
  • Control environment assessments, reporting and enhancement recommendations or implementation
